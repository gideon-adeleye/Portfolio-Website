03 — About

About Me

I'm Gideon Adeleye, a product designer based in Lagos, Nigeria. I help early-stage startups design digital products that feel clean, modern, and are built to last.

What makes me effective: I understand how things get built. With knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript fundamentals, I design with technical feasibility in mind. This means fewer "can we actually build this?" conversations and smoother collaboration with engineering teams.

Over the past 3+ years, I've designed mobile apps, SaaS dashboards, and e-commerce experiences across fintech, real estate, logistics, and more. I specialize in taking complex problems and turning them into simple, intuitive interfaces.

I thrive working with small teams and startups where design needs to move fast, make sense to developers, and solve real user problems.
